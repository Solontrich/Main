Saffron Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt, to taste
  1. Soak Saffron: Steep 1/2 teaspoon of saffron threads in 2 tablespoons of hot water for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat Ghee: In a large saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee over medium heat.
  3. Add Rice and Saffron: Add 1 cup of basmati rice and saffron mixture. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups of water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use high-quality saffron threads.
  • Adjust saffron amount for desired flavor and color.
  • Add cardamom powder, cloves, nuts, or dried fruits for extra flavor.
Coconut Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, chopped fresh cilantro
  1. Heat Oil: In a large saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil over medium heat.
  2. Add Ginger: Sauté 1 teaspoon of grated ginger for 1 minute.
  3. Add Rice: Add 1 cup of basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups of coconut milk, 1 cup of water, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use full-fat coconut milk for richer flavor.
  • Add chopped nuts, dried fruits, or shredded coconut for texture.
  • Replace coconut oil with ghee or vegetable oil.
  • Try adding spices like cinnamon, cardamom, or cloves.
Jeera Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon jeera (cumin seeds)
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, chopped fresh cilantro
  1. Heat Ghee: In a large saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee over medium heat.
  2. Temper Jeera: Add 1 teaspoon of jeera (cumin seeds) and let them sizzle for 1-2 seconds.
  3. Add Rice: Add 1 cup of basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups of water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use high-quality jeera for optimal flavor.
  • Add chopped nuts, dried fruits, or shredded coconut for texture.
  • Replace ghee with vegetable oil or coconut oil.
  • Try adding spices like cinnamon, cardamom, or cloves.
Garlic Fried Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped scallions, for garnish
  1. Heat Oil: In a large wok or frying pan, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Sauté Garlic and Onion: Add minced garlic and diced onion. Stir-fry until fragrant.
  3. Add Eggs (Optional): Pour in beaten eggs. Scramble until cooked.
  4. Add Rice: Add cooked rice, breaking clumps. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Season: Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use leftover rice to avoid mushiness.
  • Add protein like cooked chicken, shrimp or tofu.
  • Mix in chopped veggies (carrots, peas, corn).
  • Replace soy sauce with oyster sauce or sesame oil.
Lemon Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish
  1. Heat Ghee/Oil: In a large saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee/oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté Ginger: Add grated ginger. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add Rice: Add 1 cup of basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups of water, turmeric powder, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  5. Add Lemon Juice: Mix in lemon juice. Fluff rice with a fork.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use fresh lemon juice for best flavor.
  • Add chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) or dried fruits.
  • Mix in chopped fresh herbs (parsley, basil).
  • Replace ghee with coconut oil for added flavor.

Spicy Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1-2 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat Oil: In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté Onion and Ginger: Add diced onion and grated ginger. Stir-fry until onion is translucent.
  3. Add Spices: Add chili flakes, cumin, and turmeric powder. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  4. Add Rice: Add 1 cup of basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups of water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.

Tips and Variations:

  • Adjust chili flakes to desired spice level.
  • Add cooked vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers).
  • Mix in cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein.
  • Replace chili flakes with diced jalapeños or serrano peppers.
Mushroom Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, shiitake)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish
  1. Sauté Mushrooms: In a pan, melt 1 tablespoon butter over medium heat. Add mushrooms, onion, and garlic. Cook until mushrooms release moisture and start browning.
  2. Add Thyme and Rice: Add thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir in 1 cup basmati rice. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  4. Finish with Butter: Stir in remaining 1 tablespoon butter.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use variety of mushrooms for added flavor.
  • Add cooked chicken or shrimp for added protein.
  • Mix in chopped bell peppers or carrots.
  • Replace thyme with rosemary or oregano.

Bacon and Chive Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 slices of bacon, diced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  1. Cook Bacon: In a large saucepan, cook diced bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove bacon, leaving grease.
  2. Sauté Rice: Add butter, then 1 cup basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  4. Mix Bacon and Chives: Stir in cooked bacon, chopped chives, and adjust seasoning.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use smoked bacon for added flavor.
  • Add grated cheddar cheese.
  • Mix in diced onions or scallions.
  • Replace chives with parsley or thyme.
Caprese Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  1. Cook Rice: In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add 1 cup basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add Water: Add 2 cups water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  3. Mix Caprese: Fluff cooked rice with a fork. Stir in cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  4. Season: Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, and extra olive oil if needed.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use heirloom tomatoes for added color.
  • Add balsamic glaze or reduction.
  • Mix in chopped prosciutto or grilled chicken.
  • Replace basil with parsley or oregano.

Chicken and Herb Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  1. Cook Chicken: In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium-high. Add chicken; cook until browned (5-6 minutes). Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté Onion and Garlic: Reduce heat to medium. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is translucent.
  3. Add Rice and Herbs: Add 1 cup basmati rice, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add chicken broth and cooked chicken. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use fresh herbs for optimal flavor.
  • Add diced bell peppers or carrots.
  • Mix in cooked shrimp or bacon.
  • Replace rosemary with parsley or oregano.
Shrimp and Garlic Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley or scallions (optional)
  1. Cook Shrimp: In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium-high. Add shrimp; cook until pink (2-3 minutes per side). Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté Garlic: Reduce heat to medium. Add garlic; cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add Rice: Add 1 cup basmati rice, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  5. Combine Shrimp and Rice: Stir in cooked shrimp.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use fresh garlic for optimal flavor.
  • Add diced onions or bell peppers.
  • Mix in chopped fresh cilantro or parsley.
  • Replace paprika with saffron or cumin.
Roasted Vegetable Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed roasted vegetables (e.g., carrots, zucchini, bell peppers, broccoli)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  1. Roaast Vegetables: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender.
  2. Sauté Onion and Garlic: In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is translucent.
  3. Add Rice: Add 1 cup basmati rice, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  5. Combine Rice and Vegetables: Fluff cooked rice with a fork. Stir in roasted vegetables.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use variety of colorful vegetables.
  • Add cooked chicken or shrimp.
  • Mix in chopped fresh herbs (parsley, thyme, rosemary).
  • Replace thyme with paprika or cumin.
Indian-Style Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped cilantro or scallions (optional)
  1. Heat Ghee/Oil: In a large saucepan, heat ghee/oil over medium.
  2. Add Spices: Add cumin seeds; let sizzle. Add coriander, turmeric, chili powder (if using), and salt. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add Rice: Add 1 cup basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  5. Garnish: Garnish with chopped cilantro/scallions (if using).

Tips and Variations:

  • Use aged basmati rice for better flavor.
  • Add nuts (cashews, almonds), dried fruits (cranberries, raisins), or coconut flakes.
  • Mix in cooked vegetables (peas, carrots), chicken, or shrimp.
  • Replace cumin seeds with ground cumin.
Mediterranean Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed Mediterranean herbs (parsley, oregano, thyme)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Feta cheese, chopped olives (optional)
  1. Sauté Onion and Garlic: In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is translucent.
  2. Add Rice: Add 1 cup basmati rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water, herbs, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  4. Garnish: Top with feta cheese and chopped olives (if using).

Tips and Variations:

  • Add artichoke hearts, sun-dried tomatoes, or roasted red peppers.
  • Mix in cooked chicken, shrimp, or grilled halloumi cheese.
  • Replace herbs with Mediterranean spice blend.
  • Use lemon juice for extra flavor.
Mexican Rice
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup uncooked white or brown rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup diced fresh tomatoes (or 1 can, 14.5 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped cilantro, lime wedges (optional)
  1. Sauté Onion and Garlic: In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is translucent.
  2. Add Rice: Add rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add diced tomatoes, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Cook Rice: Add 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  5. Garnish: Top with chopped cilantro and serve with lime wedges (if using).

Tips and Variations:

  • Add cooked chicken, beef, or beans for added protein.
  • Mix in diced bell peppers, carrots, or zucchini.
  • Use chipotle peppers for spicy flavor.
  • Replace cumin with Mexican oregano.
