Baked Chicken in Chicken Sauce and Rice

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • (or the part of the chicken you want to use)
  • 2 cups uncooked white rice
  • 1 cup chicken sauce (see below)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)

Chicken Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Season chicken with thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high. Sear chicken until browned (5-6 minutes per side).
  4. Transfer chicken to a baking dish.
  5. Prepare chicken sauce: Combine chicken broth, heavy cream, butter, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a saucepan. Simmer over medium heat until thickened (5-7 minutes).
  6. Pour chicken sauce over baked chicken.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  8. Serve with cooked rice.

Stovetop Instructions

  1. Prepare chicken: Rinse and pat dry chicken breasts with paper towels.
  2. Season: Mix salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using). Rub evenly onto both sides of the chicken.
  3. Heat skillet: Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear chicken: Add chicken to the skillet and sear until browned (5-6 minutes per side).
  5. Add liquids: Pour in chicken broth and white wine (if using). Bring to a simmer.
  6. Cover and cook: Cover skillet with a lid. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook for 20-25 minutes or until chicken reaches 165째F (74째C).
  7. Rest: Remove from heat. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Stovetop "Baking" Method

This method mimics oven baking on the stovetop.

  1. Use a heavy-bottomed skillet (e.g., cast iron or stainless steel).
  2. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add a small amount of oil or cooking spray.
  4. Place chicken in the skillet.
  5. Cover with a lid to trap heat.

Cooking Rice

  1. Rinse 1 cup rice in a fine mesh strainer.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine rice and 2 cups water.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until tender.
  4. Fluff with a fork.

Tips and Variations

  • Use fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary for added flavor.
  • Add sliced onions, bell peppers, or mushrooms to the skillet.
  • You can Substitute chicken broth with lemon juice or chicken stock.
  • Serve with roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or rice.
