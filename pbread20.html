Classic Bread Pudding
Ingredients Instructions
  • 4 cups stale bread, cubed
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix Bread Mixture: Mix bread, sugar, eggs, cream, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl.
  3. Add Melted Butter: Pour in melted butter and mix until bread is evenly coated.
  4. Pour into Baking Dish: Pour bread mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Bake: Bake for 35-40 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Serve: Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream, if desired.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use high-quality bread for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the amount of sugar to your taste.
  • Add some dried fruit, such as cranberries or raisins, for extra flavor.
  • Try using different types of milk, such as almond or soy milk, for a non-dairy version.

Stovetop Bread Pudding
Ingredients Instructions
  • 4 cups stale bread, cubed
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  1. Melt Butter: Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Mix Bread Mixture: Mix bread, sugar, eggs, cream, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl.
  3. Cook Bread Mixture: Pour bread mixture into the saucepan with melted butter. Cook, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens and the bread is toasted, about 20-25 minutes.
  4. Serve: Serve warm, topped with your choice of toppings such as whipped cream, caramel sauce, or chopped nuts.

Tips and Variations:

  • Use high-quality bread for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the amount of sugar to your taste.
  • Add some dried fruit, such as cranberries or raisins, for extra flavor.
  • Try using different types of bread, such as baguette or ciabatta, for a unique texture.
